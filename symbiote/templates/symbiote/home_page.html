MAHATMA GANDHI NATIONAL RURAL EMPLOYMENT GUARANTEE ACT 2005

MGNREGA is a social welfare scheme in India that provides rural households with employment opportunities for 100 days a year, aimed at rural development through unskilled manual work.

VISIT
NEW OPPORTUNITIES

MGNREGA creates rural jobs, develops skills, and generates income, ultimately improving the socio-economic conditions of rural communities.

INFRASTRUCTURE DEVELOPMENT

Creating employment opportunities for rural workers in the construction of public assets such as roads, canals, and ponds. Improved infrastructure benefits rural communities.

RURAL AGRICULTURE DEVELOPMENT

Creating employment opportunities for rural workers in agriculture-related activities such as land development, irrigation projects, and horticulture. This helps to improve the rural economy and enhances food security.

WOMEN EMPOWERMENT

Focuses on empowering women by promoting gender equality and encouraging their participation in the workforce. This helps to improve the economic and social well-being of rural women.

How it works
